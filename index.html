Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused products in their original packaging. Please contact support to initiate the process.

Q: How long does shipping take?

Shipping usually takes 3-5 business days within the country and 7-14 business days for international orders.

Q: Do you offer customer support?

Yes, we have 24/7 customer support available through live chat, email, and phone.

Q: Can I track my order?

Yes, you will receive a tracking link by email once your order is shipped.

Q: Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries. Shipping times vary by location.

Q: Can I change my order after placing it?

You can make changes within 24 hours of placing the order by contacting customer service.
